Some songs can strike right at the heart, and Wu Bai's "Passive" is one of them.

"The longer I love you, the more passive I become"â€”this lyric captures the most genuine contradiction in love. When we face someone we truly love deeply, we often become overly cautious because we cherish them too much, choosing to retreat rather than take the initiative.

Wu Bai's signature raspy voice adds a unique texture to this song. Every note carries the marks of time, and every line feels like a sincere confession flowing from the depths of the heart. This is not just a song; it's a mirror that shows us our truest selves in love.

I recommend "Passive" because it uses the simplest language to interpret complex emotions. In this ever-changing world, finding a song that truly touches the heart is, in itself, a kind of happiness.
