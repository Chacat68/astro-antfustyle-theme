This Cantonese version of "Once I Thought About Ending It All" soothed the sadness within me in the gentlest way, offering the most precious hope during those difficult days.

## Song Background

"Once I Thought About Ending It All" was originally a song that deeply reflects on the meaning of life, and this Cantonese version was born under special historical circumstances. The creator wrote in the song description: "I don't want to lose anymore, so I wrote this Cantonese version of 'Once I Thought About Ending It All' for you."

This is not merely a simple language conversion, but a reinterpretation of emotion. The rhythm and emotional expression of Cantonese give this song a different warmth and texture.
