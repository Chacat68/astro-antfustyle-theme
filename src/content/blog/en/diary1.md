## one . 2021.10.23

Recently, I've been busy working overtime to meet deadlines and haven't been able to spend quality time with the people around me. My girlfriend and I celebrated our 3rd anniversary, and this year we kept it casual as usual. She took me to a new place, where we discovered an incredibly delicious restaurant. We drank, ate skewers, and all the worries and fatigue seemed to fade away with the laughter.

![pTBdej](https://blog-1259751088.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/uPic/pTBdej.tif)

Lately, I've been extremely busy, but I've been trying to carve out time to be with family and friends and adjust my life rhythm. This includes taking care of my physical well-being, and my feelings of anxiety and irritability have lessened quite a bit.

Yesterday, I visited the Pudong Art Museum, which I really like. From the outside, it's a very simple building! But once inside, I was really drawn to the texture of the architecture and the flow of movement.

![Z7X8tk](https://blog-1259751088.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/uPic/Z7X8tk.tif)

The building makes extensive use of windows, creating many viewing spaces. Observing from different angles, each perspective offers its own unique pleasure.

![nLFsIv](https://blog-1259751088.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/uPic/nLFsIv.tif)

Through a window, I captured the afternoon sunlight casting dappled spots on the pure white walls, echoing the theme on a poster. This is my favorite photo from recent times.

![FDeQzw](https://blog-1259751088.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/uPic/FDeQzw.tif)

## two 20211030

A friend invited me to a birthday party. We happily chatted, cooked together, caught up on each other's recent lives, and discussed industry trends. It felt really nice to reunite with old friends.

![lyS7U7](https://blog-1259751088.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/uPic/lyS7U7.tif)

While waiting for a ride, I saw BMW's Halloween market. The people participating in the market activities had joyful smiles on their faces, which quickly infected me and made me genuinely happy from the heart.

![aYdwIr](https://blog-1259751088.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/uPic/aYdwIr.tif)
