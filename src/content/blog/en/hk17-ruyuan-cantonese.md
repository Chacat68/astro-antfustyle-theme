## Creative Background

Since 2019, "Not the Lyric God" has held deep love for the motherland and profound reverence for the sacrifices of the martyrs. The hardest part of creating such songs is making people understand that the current peaceful and prosperous era did not come easily. Every ordinary day is a precious gift bought with the lives of the martyrs.

This Cantonese version of "As Wished" was created with precisely such emotions. It is not just a song but a heartfelt confession of love for the motherland and a lofty tribute to the martyrs.

## Musical Features

The Cantonese version of "As Wished" maintains the original song's deep emotional tone while incorporating the unique rhythmic beauty of Cantonese. Tangtang YuzeTang's performance is heartfelt and infectious, expressing the song's sentiments of home and country to the fullest.

The theme in the lyrics, "Cantonese songs for the mountains and rivers, a small reunion in a prosperous era," reflects both the characteristics of Cantonese culture and expresses deep longing for the motherland's unity and the people's reunion.
