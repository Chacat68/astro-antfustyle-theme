> Hong Kong Music Collection: Recounting those eras of Cantonese songs intertwined with light and shadow, recording unforgettable melodies in our hearts, sharing stories of Hong Kong music, whether popular or niche.

In December 2025, a heartbreaking fire occurred in Hong Kong, shocking the world.

Upon learning this news, "Not the Lyric God" and Sister Xin decided to send their heartfelt wishes to our Hong Kong compatriots through music. As the creators said: "Such immense pain is incredibly difficult to soothe. But perhaps we can offer a song, a gesture of care, and an embrace."

A song titled "Impermanence" speaks of the ever-changing world and the impermanence of life. In the face of disaster, humanity seems so small, yet love and warmth can transcend everything. This song is not merely a cover; it is a profound expression of care and blessing from mainland compatriots to Hong Kong.
