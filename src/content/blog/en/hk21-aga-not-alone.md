## Preface

Some songs, when sung by a different voice, take on a whole new emotion.

Dear Jane's original version of "No One Is Doomed to Be Alone" is a straightforward rock-style confession: the guitar and drums are full, and the emotion surges forward. But when AGA (Jiang Haijia) takes it on, she doesn't replicate that explosive power. Instead, she sings the same promise more intimately, more quietly. The original love that "wants the whole world to hear" becomes a softly whispered "I'm here" right by your ear.

This cover comes from a live performance in 2019. The stage is restrained, with no complex effects—just a spotlight, a microphone, and the voice itself. And precisely because the arrangement is simple, the emotional expression becomes even clearer.

## About AGA

AGA (Jiang Haijia), born in 1988, is a Hong Kong singer-songwriter of Spanish descent.

She grew up in a musical environment: both her parents were musicians, and she started learning guitar and piano at an early age. Before her official debut, AGA had already written songs for artists like Hacken Lee, Fiona Sit, and Kary Ng. In 2013, she entered the public eye with her original work "One," which became a champion song on all four major Hong Kong radio stations.

In 2020, she won the Gold Award for Female Singer and the Gold Award for Singer-Songwriter at the Ultimate Song Chart Awards. In 2023, AGA held her first solo concert, "ONEDERFUL LIVE," at the Hong Kong Coliseum, allowing more listeners to see the fruits of her years of hard work.

AGA's voice is clear but not airy; it sounds gentle yet has a solid foundation. She doesn't rely on vocal acrobatics to drive emotion but instead uses her articulation and breath to gradually bring out the intimacy in the song. This is the most significant difference between her cover and the original.
