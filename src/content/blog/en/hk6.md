Yesterday, while watching the show "Infinity and Beyond - Greater Bay Area," I saw Brother Jordan Chan perform this song, and it still moved me deeply. This song is one of the tracks I have on repeat in my playlist and is also one of the few sweet songs in his repertoire. The lyrics were written by Wyman, and the music was composed by Chan Kwong Wing. The entire song is full of emotional power, and every time I listen to it, I can't help but get teary-eyed.

The moment that made me fall in love with this song was the performance version from the 2015 "Young and Dangerous: Friendship Over Time Concert." During the performance, his sweet interaction with his wife, Ding Ding, created a classic and memorable scene in Cantonese music.
