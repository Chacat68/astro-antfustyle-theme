## About This Song

"Deep Slumber" represents Cyndi Wang's exploration of "maturity" and "depth" in her recent musical creations. In this fast-paced era, she uses this song to invite us to press pause togetherâ€”to contemplate in this "deep slumber" who we are, what we have missed, and how we can start anew.
