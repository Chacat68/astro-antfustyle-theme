> Chinese Collection: Recording the Chinese music that accompanied my growth, sharing melodies and lyrics that touch the heart.

Anpu is a singer I deeply admire. Her interpretation of life and songs influenced me greatly in my youth. Recently, I stumbled upon her video for "About I Love You" on YouTube and found it very moving, so I decided to share it with you all.

In my younger days, I understood the lyrics of this song as longing for a lover, but now I feel it's more about longing for oneself.
