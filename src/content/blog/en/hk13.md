## Preface

In the world of love, we have all experienced such moments: the person who was once the most important can only appear as a "guest" at the other's significant life events. That sense of distance, that feeling of alienation, is more cruel than any words.

Yet, it is precisely this regret that makes love more precious. It is precisely this distance that makes longing more profound. "Guest" tells the most painful story in the gentlest way, and comforts every wounded heart with the most beautiful melody.
