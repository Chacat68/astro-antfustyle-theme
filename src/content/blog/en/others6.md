## Transcript

As I began to love myself

I found that anguish and emotional suffering are only warning signs that I was living against my own truth.

Today, I know, this is

"AUTHENTICITY".

When I truly began to love myself,
I realized that all pain and emotional torment
were merely reminders: to live without betraying my own heart.
Today I understand, this is called
'Authenticity'.

As I began to love myself

I understood how much it can offend somebody as I try to force my desires on this person,

even though I knew the time was not right and the person was not ready for it,

and even though this person was me.

Today I call it

"RESPECT".

When I truly began to love myself,
I learned how rude it is to impose my wishes on others,
even when I knew the timing wasn't right,
and the person wasn't ready,
even if that person was myself.
Today I understand, this is called
'Respect'.

As I began to love myself

I stopped craving for a different life,

and I could see that everything that surrounded me was inviting me to grow.

Today I call it

"MATURITY".

When I began to love myself,
I stopped craving a different life,
and I could see that everything happening around me
was an invitation for me to grow.
Now, I call it
'Maturity'.

As I began to love myself

I understood that at any circumstance,

I am in the right place at the right time,

and everything happens at the exactly right moment,

so I could be calm.

Today I call it

"SELF-CONFIDENCE".

When I truly began to love myself,
I understood that I have always been
in the right place, at the right time, and everything happened just as it should.
Thus, I found peace.
Today I understand, this is called
'Self-Confidence'.

As I began to love myself

I quit steeling my own time,

and I stopped designing huge projects for the future.

Today, I only do what brings me joy and happiness,

things I love to do and that make my heart cheer,

and I do them in my own way

and in my own rhythm.

Today I call it

"HONESTY".

When I truly began to love myself,
I stopped sacrificing my free time,
and I stopped sketching grand plans for tomorrow.
Today I only do what is fun and joyful,
things I love that make my heart sing,
in my own way, at my own rhythm.
Today I understand, this is called
'Simplicity'.

"LOVE OF ONESELF".

I started distancing myself from anything unhealthy.
Whether it was food, people, things, or situations,
I stepped away from anything that pulled me away from my true self.
I used to call this "pursuing healthy selfishness,"
but today I understand, this is
'Self-Love'.

I quit trying to always be right,

"MODESTY".

"PERFECTION".

where EVERYTHING is happening.
Today, I live here and now,

'Perfection'.

As I began to love myself

Today I call this connection

WISDOM OF THE HEART.

This combination I call,
'Wisdom of the Heart'.
